ABOUT ME

I design digital experiences that connect business goals with real user needs.

As a Freelance Web Designer, I collaborate directly with clients to gather requirements and deliver user-focused solutions. My work involves designing and developing landing pages and web platforms, applying advanced UI/UX principles to ensure seamless user experiences and cohesive interfaces. By translating visual concepts into functional products, I strive to create designs that align with client objectives.

With a degree in Computer Systems Engineering from Instituto Tecnológico de Tijuana, I specialize in UI/UX design and consulting. My commitment to continuous learning includes expanding my technical skills to enhance communication between design and development teams. My goal is to bridge the gap between user needs and digital solutions while contributing to impactful projects.
