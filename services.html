FAQ

What is your typical design process?

+

My process starts with understanding your goals and users through research. Then I define structure, create wireframes, and move into visual design. Finally, I iterate based on feedback to refine the experience.

How long does a project take?

+

It depends on the scope. A landing page can take 3–5 days, while a full product design may take several weeks. I always provide a clear timeline before starting.

Do you also develop websites?

+

Yes. I develop landing pages and templates using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Bootstrap. For larger platforms, I focus on design and collaborate with developers.

Can you work with existing products?

+

Absolutely. I can audit your current product, identify usability issues, and redesign specific parts or the entire experience.

How do we start working together?

+

Just send me a message with your idea or project details. I’ll review it and get back to you with next steps and a proposal.
